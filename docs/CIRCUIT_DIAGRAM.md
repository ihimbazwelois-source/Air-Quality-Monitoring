# Smart Air Quality Monitoring System - Circuit Diagram

## System Schematic

We used **Proteus** to draw the schematic circuit diagram for our Smart Air Quality Monitoring System.

![Circuit Schematic Diagram](circuit_schematic.jpg)

### Circuit Design Overview

The schematic shows the complete electronic circuit architecture including:

- **Microcontroller Unit (MCU)** - Central processing unit for data collection and processing
- **MQ135 Gas Sensors** - Multiple sensor nodes positioned at different locations
- **DHT11/DHT22 Temperature & Humidity Sensors** - Environmental data collection
- **Communication Modules** - Wireless connectivity (RF modules or WiFi)
- **Power Supply Circuit** - Battery and power management
- **Signal Conditioning** - Amplification and filtering circuits

### Tools Used

- **Software**: Proteus Design Suite
- **Purpose**: Professional schematic design and simulation

### Schematic Details

The circuit diagram illustrates:
1. **Sensor Input Stage** - Multiple MQ135 sensors for air quality measurement
2. **Signal Processing** - Analog-to-Digital Conversion (ADC) circuits
3. **Control Logic** - Microcontroller-based decision making
4. **Output Stage** - Alert mechanisms and data transmission
5. **Power Management** - Voltage regulation and battery management
